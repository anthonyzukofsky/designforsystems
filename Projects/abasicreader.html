A BASIC READER
“A text is made of multiple writings... The reader is the space on which all the quotations that make up a writing are inscribed; a text’s unity lies not in its origins but in its destination.” —Roland Barthes, “The Death of the Author,” Aspen, No. 5–6, 1967

QUESTION
How can you collaborate to build an object that will influence future students?

An article or essay is a written work published in print or electronic medium It may be used for the purpose of spreading news, research results, academic analysis or debates. The aim of the project is to design a book as a compendium of essays/articles/poems/images that document your time as a student to help future students.
